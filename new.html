🔬 Why It Matters — Research Facts
Data from peer-reviewed studies and global agencies
🐋
8 Million
Metric tonnes of plastic enter our oceans every year — equivalent to dumping a garbage truck of plastic every minute.
Source: Jambeck et al., Science 2015
🐢
700+ Species
Marine species are negatively impacted by plastic debris. Sea turtles mistake plastic bags for jellyfish; 100,000+ marine mammals die annually.
Source: IUCN Red List 2022
💰
₹8 Lakh Cr
Annual economic damage to marine industries including tourism, fishing, and shipping from marine plastic pollution globally.
Source: UNEP 2021 Economic Analysis
🇮🇳
0.6 MT/yr
India contributes approximately 0.6 million tonnes of plastic to ocean pollution annually, with coastlines spanning 7,516 km.
Source: Ocean Conservancy India 2023
🔁
95% Savings
Recycling plastic uses 95% less energy than producing virgin plastic from petroleum. Every kg recycled = 5.77 kWh saved.
Source: US EPA Energy Recovery Report
🥅
640,000 T
Ghost fishing gear (abandoned nets & lines) accounts for 640,000 tonnes of ocean plastic annually, trapping 136,000 marine mammals.
Source: WWF Fishing Gear Report 2021
